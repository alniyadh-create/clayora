Shipping Policy

We ship across India with trusted courier partners. Below are the important details regarding shipping and delivery.

Dispatch & Delivery

Orders are dispatched within the timeline shown on the product or order confirmation page — typically within 2–5 business days depending on stock and demand.

Once dispatched, you will receive tracking details via email/SMS. Delivery time depends on the courier and destination.

Shipping Charges

Shipping charges (if any) are calculated at checkout. We may offer free shipping promotions from time to time.

Fragile Items

Our products are handcrafted ceramics and are inherently fragile. While we pack carefully, Clayora is not responsible for breakages that occur during courier transit. We recommend recording a full unboxing video at delivery — this helps with any manufacturing-defect claims.

Delays & Force Majeure

Clayora is not liable for delivery delays due to courier performance, weather, strikes, or other events beyond our control.
