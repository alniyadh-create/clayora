Cancellation & Refund Policy

Thank you for shopping with Clayora. Please read our cancellation & refund policy carefully before placing an order.

Cancellation

Orders may be cancelled only before dispatch. If you wish to cancel, contact us immediately after placing your order. Once an order has been dispatched, cancellation is not possible.

Refunds & Replacements

Refunds and replacements are available only for confirmed manufacturing defects. To be eligible for replacement or refund you must provide a full unboxing video showing the package being opened and the product condition as soon as you receive it.

  • Without a clear unboxing video we cannot accept claims for manufacturing defects.
  • If a manufacturing defect is confirmed, we will offer a replacement or refund at our discretion.

Shipping Damage

Because our products are made of ceramic and are fragile by nature, Clayora is not liable for damage that occurs during shipping or courier handling. We strongly recommend inspecting the package in the presence of the delivery agent and recording an unboxing video.

Important

By purchasing from Clayora you acknowledge and accept that online purchases of fragile ceramic items carry risk. We recommend purchasing in-person if you are concerned about transit damage.
