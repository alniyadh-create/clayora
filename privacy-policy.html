Privacy Policy

Clayora is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what personal information we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide (name, email, address, phone), order details, and communication history. We may also collect analytics about your use of the website.

How We Use Information

We use your information to process orders, communicate about your order, provide customer service, and improve our services. We may share necessary details with courier partners for delivery.

Third Parties

We do not sell or rent personal data. We may share information with trusted service providers (payment processors, couriers) only to fulfill orders.

Security

We follow reasonable security practices to protect personal data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.
